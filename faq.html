Before you ask, we already answered.

How is Exoo faster than other AI assistants?+

Most assistants optimize for answer quality first and speed second. Exoo's pipeline is tuned the other way: it starts streaming a useful answer within milliseconds, then keeps refining as more context loads.

Can I trust the answers?+

Every answer comes with inline source citations you can click to verify. If Exoo isn't confident, it says so instead of guessing.

Can I upload my own documents?+

Yes. Pro and Team plans let you upload docs, PDFs, or connect a codebase, and Exoo will answer using that context for the rest of the conversation.

Is there a free plan?+

Yes. Starter gives you 50 questions a month at no cost, no credit card required.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time — you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Does Exoo work in other languages?+

Yes. Ask in whichever language you're comfortable with — Exoo answers in that same language by default.

What happens if I go over my Starter limit?+

You'll get a heads-up in the app once you're close. You can upgrade to Pro at any point to keep going without interruption.
